Welcome to Queen Bee Café

About us

This is the story of a brave woman that liked Coffee, but much more she loved Bees. She dreamed about the idea of opening a little Café where the clients could feel at home and also learn how important bees are in this world.
That dream came true and she named her little treasure "Queen Bee Café".
We are very proud to offer handmade food using organic ingredients from local Farmers helping our community.

Don't leave without visiting our shop where you can find only quality and unique handmade products.

Bee Lovers

Our passion for Bees is huge and we have been working very hard to get an important number of hives in our garden. This is our little contribution to avoid the extinction of this amazing insect.
What they do for us is priceless giving us
delicious Honey and pollinating
our plants and get healthy vegetables and fruits.
We want to show everyone their fantastic world organizing a Beekeeping Course
for small groups almost every month
(remember, they are sleeping in the winter season).
