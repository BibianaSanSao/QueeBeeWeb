About us

This is the story of a brave woman that liked Coffee, but much more she loved Bees. She dreamed with the idea of opening a little Café where the clients could feel like home and also learn how important bees are in this world.
That dream came true and she named her little treasure "Queen Bee Café".
We are very proud to offer handmade food using organic ingredients from local Farmers helping to our comunity.

Are you hungry?

Don't leave without visiting our shop where you can find only quality and unics handmade products.

Visit our Shop

Our passion for Bees is huge and we have been working very hard to get an important number of hives in our garden. This is our little contribution to avoid the extinguintion of this amazing insect.
What they do for us is priceless giving us a delicious Honey and pollinating our plants and get healthy vegetables and fruits.

We want to show everyone their fantastic world organising a Bee Keeping Course for small groups almost every month (remember they are sleeping in winter season)
