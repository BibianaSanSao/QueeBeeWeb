Save the Bees


Do you know how important are Bees to our planet?

These little criatures have been in Planet Earth for millions of years, working very hard. They are one of the most important pollinators. Thanks to this we can get delicious vegetables and sweet fruit. Also the bees produce a sweet golden syrup called Honey. This sticky liquid is famous not only for its sweet taste, but also for its medicinal properties
